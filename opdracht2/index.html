Hi, my name is Chevelly Hiralall

...and I study the course Communication & Multimedia Design. At CMD you learn to research, to invent and to create meaningful interactive, digital products and services such as websites, apps and interactive installations by involving the wishes, needs and characteristics of users in the design process. During my course at the Hogeschool van Amsterdam I did a lot of individual and team projects that helped me to reflect on my own strengths and weaknesses. My ambition is to become part of a creative team that believes in the power of ideas. My aim is to bring these ideas to life through the process of Creative Production. I believe that great ideas come from different minds and I like to work in a team, where everyone puts their own creativity in. Inspired by arts, culture, science and technology, I seek to create new ways of interaction.
